Monad Ecosystem

The Monad ecosystem is growing rapidly. From DeFi protocols and NFT platforms to wallet integrations and infrastructure providers, Monad is home to a vibrant community of builders and users.

DeFi Protocols

Monad's high throughput and 1-second block times make it the ideal platform for decentralized finance. Automated market makers (AMMs), lending protocols, and perpetuals exchanges all benefit from the ability to process thousands of transactions per second with deterministic low latency. Liquidity providers enjoy faster settlement, reduced slippage, and lower gas costs compared to slower EVM chains. The parallel execution engine means complex DeFi transactions — flash loans, multi-hop swaps — can be processed without the bottlenecks seen on Ethereum mainnet.

DEX / AMM

Automated market makers leverage Monad's speed for tight spreads, rapid price discovery, and MEV-resistant order routing at scale.

Lending & Borrowing

Money markets benefit from instant liquidations and rapid oracle updates, reducing protocol risk and improving capital efficiency for lenders.

Perpetuals

On-chain perpetuals exchanges require high throughput for order matching and position management — a natural fit for Monad's parallel architecture.

NFT Platforms & Gaming

Monad's low gas costs and high throughput enable new categories of NFT experiences and fully on-chain games that would be prohibitively expensive on Ethereum mainnet. ERC-721 and ERC-1155 standards are fully supported, allowing NFT collections to deploy without any code changes.

NFT Marketplaces

Trade, mint, and auction NFTs with near-instant confirmation and minimal gas fees. All standard NFT token standards (ERC-721, ERC-1155) work immediately on Monad.

On-Chain Games

Fully on-chain game state with 1-second block times enables real-time gameplay, turn-based strategies, and interactive experiences powered entirely by smart contracts.

Wallet Support

Any EVM-compatible wallet supports Monad by adding a custom network. MetaMask is the most widely used wallet and connects to Monad via a simple network configuration. WalletConnect-compatible wallets also work out of the box.

MetaMask — Add Monad Mainnet
  1. Open MetaMask → Settings → Networks → Add a network
  2. Enter Network Name: Monad
  3. Enter RPC URL: your Dwellir endpoint
  4. Enter Chain ID: 143
  5. Enter Currency Symbol: MON
  6. Enter Block Explorer: https://monadexplorer.com
  7. Save and switch to Monad
